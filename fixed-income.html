Fixed Income Fund

Read More...

The Fixed Income Fund offers you the benefit of an investment in Naira bonds issued by Government and corporates. The fund is appropriate for investors looking for growth without taking too much risk.

The objective is to achieve competitive returns on investments with moderate risk by investing in high quality bonds, commercial papers, fixed deposits and treasury bills. Liquidation before expiration of the holding period attracts a 10% penalty fee on accrued interest.

Benefits and features of Bond Fund:

  • Start with a minimum of N10,000
  • High quality fixed income instruments
  • Minimum holding period of 90 days
  • Open-ended funds
  • Free entry and exit
  • No withholding tax and no VAT on dividends
  • Online access to your portfolio
  • Professional expertise of Fund Manager
  • Opportunity to invest on behalf of minors
