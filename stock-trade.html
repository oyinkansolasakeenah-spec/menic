A Securities Trading account provides direct market access to enable one trade on the floor of the Nigeria Exchange from the comfort of your smartphone or computer.

The objective is to achieve competitive returns on investments with moderate risk by investing in high quality bonds, commercial papers, fixed deposits and treasury bills. Liquidation before expiration of the holding period attracts a 10% penalty fee on accrued interest.

Benefits and features of Securities Trading:

  • Zero amount needed for account opening.
  • Daily market reports
  • Weekly stock recommendation reports
  • Timely and efficient execution of mandates
  • Share registration services which include share certificate management, dividend management, transfer of share and handling of registrars related issues
  • Immediate contract notes on transactions
  • Online access to stock broking account both trading and portfolio statements.
  • Online funding of account, one-off or recurrent funding
  • Real time stock recommendation for the stocks we cover
