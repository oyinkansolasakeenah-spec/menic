Nigerian Diaspora Trusts

Read More...

The Diaspora Trust is designed to provide secure, high fidelity investing options for Nigerians in the diaspora including fund investments in Naira and Dollar-denominated instruments and acquisition of all types of assets.

The objective is to achieve competitive returns on investments with moderate risk by investing in high quality bonds, commercial papers, fixed deposits and treasury bills. Liquidation before expiration of the holding period attracts a 10% penalty fee on accrued interest.

Benefits and features of Nigerian Diaspora Trust:

  • Confidentiality of the Trust and safety of assets are duly preserved.
  • Activities undertaken under the Trust are subject to a pre-agreed investment template that would be drawn up in a legally binding document called a Trust deed.
  • Competitive return as investment will be managed by a team of investment experts.
  • Performance reporting as the settlor is kept abreast through periodic reporting on the investment on bi-annual basis.
  • For real estate investments (Land purchase or building construction) such will be undertaken and carefully supervised by UCT leveraging on other professionals and accredited business partners.
  • Option of a lump sum or flexible fixed contributions over time, or even a standing payment order through your bank.
  • Funds may be availed directly to the beneficiary or third party to prevent diversion of funds.
